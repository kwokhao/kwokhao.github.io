Singapore Initiative on Digital Economics

The rise of digital platforms and artificial intelligence has raised concerns regarding their impact on society, business, and the global economy. The Singapore Initiative on Digital Economics seeks to address these concerns by bringing research to practice.

We aim to foster collaboration between industry professionals and scholars from business and allied disciplines, including computer science, economics, law, etc. Our goal is to generate valuable insights and share them with a diverse audience, including academics, business leaders, and the public.

Focus areas

  • Analyse innovation, disruption, and competition within online platforms and attendant ecosystems
  • Assess how regulation impacts platform competition and value creation
  • Review policy recommendations from legislators, think tanks, industry associations, and consumer advocacy groups
  • Explore the impact of artificial intelligence on product and labour markets, consumer privacy, and related issues

Please stay tuned for further details.
