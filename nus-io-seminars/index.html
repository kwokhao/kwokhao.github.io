2025 NUS IO Day

  • Date: August 15, 2025
  • Time: 12.45pm-6.05pm
  • Location: Mochtar Riady Building (BIZ1), Room 06-04
  • Guests of Honour: Chenyu Yang (Maryland)

The National University of Singapore will hold its second Industrial Organisation Day on 15th August, 2025. The conference co-chairs in 2025 are Richard Grice (NUS Marketing) and Kwok Hao Lee (NUS Strategy and Policy).

Time Programme
12:45 Welcome
12:50 Session 1
Yuting Zhu (NUS Marketing)
“Generative Search: Evidence from a Large-Scale Field Experiment”

Zhonglin Li (NUS Real Estate)
“Vertical Control and Retail Competition: Evidence from the Consumer Battery Industry”
14:25 Coffee Break
14:40 Session 2
Richard Grice (NUS Marketing)
“Learning Competitors’ Identities from the Timing of Pricing Decisions: An Application to Retail Gasoline”

Ruiqi Wang (NUS Economics)
“Connect or Clique? The Role of Agent Networks in the Housing Market”
16:15 Coffee Break
16:30 Session 3
Fei Teng (NUS Marketing)
“Long-Term Effects of Early Rater Mix on Platform Fairness and Quality”

TBC
18:05 Adjourn
TBC Dinner (Speakers and Guests of Honour)

NUS IO+ Seminars

The National University of Singapore (NUS) regularly hosts seminar speakers in all fields of Economics, including but not limited to Empirical Industrial Organization (and IO-plus). See

  • https://bschool.nus.edu.sg/strategy-policy/events/ for all seminars at the Department of Strategy and Policy;
  • https://bschool.nus.edu.sg/real-estate/events/ for all seminars at the Department of Real Estate; and
  • https://fass.nus.edu.sg/ecs/seminar-this-semester/ for all seminars at the Department of Economics.
